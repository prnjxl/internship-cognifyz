Program Details

The Cognifyz Technologies Web Developer Internship is a 12-week program designed to provide aspiring web developers with practical experience in the field. Interns will work alongside experienced professionals on real-world projects.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for this internship program, candidates must:

  • Be currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a degree program in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field
  • Possess basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Be able to commit to the full 6-week duration of the program
  • Have strong communication skills and ability to work in a team environment

Application Process

The application process consists of the following steps:

  1. Online application submission
  2. Technical assessment (coding test)
  3. Interview with the technical team
  4. Final selection notification

Program Structure

The internship is structured as follows:

  • Week 1: Orientation and fundamentals training
  • Week 2-3: Shadowing and assisted project work
  • Week 4-5: Independent project work with mentorship
  • Week 6: Project presentation and evaluation

Stipend and Benefits

Selected interns will receive:

  • A monthly stipend based on performance and skills
  • Certificate of completion upon successful program conclusion
  • Opportunity for full-time employment based on performance
  • Access to learning resources and professional development workshops

Intellectual Property

All work created during the internship period is the intellectual property of Cognifyz Technologies. Interns may include the projects in their portfolios with appropriate attribution and approval.

Code of Conduct

Interns are expected to adhere to Cognifyz Technologies' code of conduct, which includes:

  • Professional behavior and communication
  • Punctuality and adherence to deadlines
  • Respect for colleagues and company property
  • Confidentiality regarding company information and client data

Termination Policy

Cognifyz Technologies reserves the right to terminate the internship if:

  • The intern fails to meet performance expectations
  • The intern violates the code of conduct
  • The intern is unable to complete the required hours
  • Business circumstances necessitate program changes

Additional Policies

Interns must comply with all company policies regarding:

  • Information security and data protection
  • Use of company resources and equipment
  • Remote work protocols (if applicable)
  • Health and safety guidelines

Performance Evaluation

Intern performance will be evaluated based on:

  • Technical skill development
  • Project contributions and quality of work
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Communication and teamwork
  • Adaptability and learning capacity

Certificate and References

Upon successful completion of the internship, interns will receive:

  • Official certificate of internship completion
  • Performance evaluation report
  • Letter of recommendation (upon request and based on performance)

Program Modifications

Cognifyz Technologies reserves the right to modify any aspect of the internship program, including duration, structure, and content, to better align with business needs and educational objectives.

Feedback Mechanism

Interns will have regular opportunities to provide feedback on their experience and receive constructive feedback on their performance through:

  • Weekly check-ins with mentors
  • Mid-program evaluation
  • End-of-program survey and exit interview
